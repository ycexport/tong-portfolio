Calm the Chaos

Calm the Chaos

Ashwaganda Commercial

  • Tools: After Effects, Cinema 4D, Procreate.
  • Role: Concept, Design, Animation.

This is a speculative spot centered on Ashwagandha, a herbal supplement which helps with anxiety and stress. Stress is depicted with repetitive, heavy, chaotic visuals; a 3D, herb-inspired capsule enters as the pivot, and the scene resolves into smooth, clear, quiet moments that read as calm.

Process

I first planned shape the words into label and capsules. After a few tests, I realized it took viewers too long to recognize them, so I switched to simple, emoji-like icons to convey uneasse at a glance.
